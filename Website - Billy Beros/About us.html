The Small Things Become Big Things


When you move about your life, it's innevitable
that you won't be able to water your plants. Amongst the common problems of life; work, food, bills,
watering the plants seems small. However, it all adds
up.

The Small Things Become Nonexistent Things


Trying to make the small problems small again with
schedules and plans is just making them big things.
What you really need is somebody to just do what you
can't do.

Robots now water your plants!
